# fuxem User Flows & Privacy Controls

## Core User Flows

### 1. Onboarding & Registration
- Invite-only or passcode-based entry
- Multi-step onboarding (pronouns, gender, interests, intentions, privacy preferences)
- Profile creation with crossdresser-centric options

### 2. Profile Management
- Edit profile (bio, photos, privacy settings)
- Set profile visibility: public, members-only, private
- Manage connections (friends, followers, blocks)

### 3. Discovery & Search
- Browse/search by interests, location, kinks, relationship type
- Filter by online status, verified profiles, etc.

### 4. Chat & Video Rooms
- Join public or private rooms
- Consent-driven private messaging and video calls
- Report/block users from chat interface

### 5. Groups & Classifieds
- Join/create groups (public/private)
- Post/view classifieds (with moderation)

### 6. Safety & Moderation
- Report/block users anywhere on the platform
- Community guidelines visible and enforced
- Moderation dashboard for admins

---

## Privacy & Safety Controls
- Profile visibility settings (public, members-only, private)
- Consent required for private messages/video
- Option to hide/show online status
- Block/report features everywhere
- Invite-only or passcode onboarding (default)
- Moderation tools for content and user management
- Clear community guidelines and enforcement

---

These flows and controls should be reviewed and refined as the platform grows and user feedback is collected.
