# fuxem Feature Roadmap

This roadmap prioritizes features for fuxem, inspired by Fetlife, Squirt.org, and Adult FriendFinder, but tailored for crossdressers and their admirers.

## Phase 1: Core Platform (MVP)
- Invite-only onboarding (passcode or invite code)
- User profiles with privacy controls (visibility, pronouns, interests, etc.)
- Real-time chat rooms (video, audio, text)
- Consent-driven private messaging
- Basic search & discovery (by interests, location, etc.)
- Community guidelines and moderation/reporting tools

## Phase 2: Community & Engagement
- Groups and communities (public/private)
- Classifieds/ads (with moderation)
- Advanced search filters (kinks, relationship types, etc.)
- Profile verification (optional)
- Event listings (virtual or in-person)

## Phase 3: Premium & Growth
- Premium features (boosts, advanced filters, private albums)
- Payment integration (if monetization desired)
- Mobile app support
- Analytics and feedback tools

---

## Guiding Principles
- Safety, privacy, and consent are top priorities
- Designed for crossdressers and those seeking meaningful, discreet connections
- Moderation and reporting tools are essential from day one

---

This roadmap should be revisited and refined as the project evolves and user feedback is collected.
